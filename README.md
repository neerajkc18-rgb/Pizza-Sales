# Pizza-Sales
The Pizza Sales Report Dashboard is an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard developed to analyze pizza sales performance, customer ordering behavior, and product category trends. The dashboard provides valuable insights into sales patterns, peak ordering times, pizza preferences, and overall business performance.

Built using Power BI, this dashboard enables businesses to monitor KPIs, identify top-performing categories, and make data-driven decisions to improve sales and customer satisfaction.

Business Insights
Weekend days, especially Friday and Saturday, receive the highest number of orders.
July and January recorded peak monthly sales.
The Classic pizza category contributes the highest share of total sales and orders.
Large-sized pizzas generate the maximum sales contribution.
Monthly trend analysis helps identify customer demand patterns and sales fluctuations.
