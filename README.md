The Best/worst Pizza 
This dashboard is a Pizza Sales Report Dashboard designed to analyze pizza sales performance over a selected time period. It provides insights into revenue, order quantity, customer demand, and product performance using charts and KPI cards.

Overview

The report summarizes pizza sales data from January 1, 2015 to November 16, 2015. It allows filtering by Pizza Category and date range to evaluate business performance interactively.

Dashboard Components
1. Header Section
Displays the title “Pizza Sales Report”
Includes:
Pizza Category Filter dropdown
Date Range Slider for selecting a reporting period

These controls help users analyze sales for specific categories and timeframes.

2. KPI (Key Performance Indicator) Cards

The top section contains summary metrics:

Total Revenue
Displays overall sales revenue generated.
Indicates total business earnings during the selected period.
Average Order Value
Shows the average amount spent per order.
Helps evaluate customer spending behavior.
Total Pizzas Sold
Total quantity of pizzas sold.
Total Orders
Number of customer orders placed.
Average Pizzas per Order
Measures how many pizzas customers purchase in a single order on average.

These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of overall business performance.

3. Best / Worst Seller Panel

Located on the left side, this section highlights:

Best Sellers

Shows pizzas contributing the highest:

Revenue
Quantity sold
Number of orders

Example:

The Thai Chicken Pizza generates maximum revenue.
The Classic Deluxe Pizza contributes highest quantity and orders.
Worst Sellers

Shows pizzas with the lowest:

Revenue
Quantity sold
Orders

Example:

The Brie Carre Pizza contributes minimum revenue and orders.
The Bare Carre Pizza contributes minimum quantity sold.

This helps identify:

Top-performing products
Underperforming menu items
Opportunities for menu optimization
4. Top 5 Analysis Charts

The dashboard contains three charts showing best-performing pizzas:

Top 5 Pizzas by Revenue
Displays pizzas generating the most income.
Useful for identifying profitable products.
Top 5 Pizzas by Quantity
Shows pizzas sold in highest quantities.
Indicates customer favorites.
Top 5 Pizzas by Total Orders
Highlights pizzas ordered most frequently.
Measures popularity across customer transactions.

These charts help management understand:

Customer preferences
High-demand products
Revenue-driving menu items
5. Bottom 5 Analysis Charts

The lower section highlights low-performing pizzas:

Bottom 5 Pizzas by Revenue
Lowest earning pizzas.

These insights support:
